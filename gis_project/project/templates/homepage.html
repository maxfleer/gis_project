IN THE CAPTIVATING WORLD OF 'MAP MANIA' PLAYERS ARE INVITED TO TEST THEIR GEOGRAPHICAL KNOWLEDGE AND INTUITION. THE GAME BEGINS BY PRESENTING BEAUTIFUL PHOTOS OF A FAMOUS LANDMARK OR PARK FROM AROUND THE WORLD TO THE PLAYER. THESE CAPTIVATING IMAGES PROVIDE THEM WITH A VISUAL GLIMPSE OF THE LOCATION THEY WILL NEED TO IDENTIFY.
THEN THE CHALLENGE BEGINS. PLAYERS ARE TRANSPORTED TO A GLOBAL GEOGRAPHICAL MAP AND THEIR TASK IS TO LOCATE THE EXACT POSITION OF THE LANDMARK IN QUESTION.
